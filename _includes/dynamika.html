Dynamika Open Access

Free edition of the leading cloud-based platform for standardised analysis of static and contrast-enhanced MRI.

No cost - full functionality

Dynamika Open Access is a fully featured, free edition of Dynamika Enterprise that handles up to 20 MRI studies. Save time-consuming manual analysis and get the most out of your MRI data.

State-of-the-art algorithms

Apply automatic patient motion correction, calculate voxel-by-voxel based contrast uptake curves and visualize parametric maps on top of your anatomical images from wherever you want in your browser.

Improved workflow

Improved MRI Reading Workflow: Retrieve images from PACS, draw Regions of Interest (ROIs) and obtain automatically calculated, sensitive and reproducible imaging biomarkers. Enhance MRI reporting through easy PDF, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel file creation.
