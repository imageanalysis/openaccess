Dynamika Open Access

Free edition of the leading cloud-based platform for standardised analysis of static and contrast-enhanced MRI.

No cost - full functionality

Dynamika Open Access is a fully featured, zero-cost version of Dynamika Enterprise that handles up to 20 MRI studies. Save time-consuming manual labor and get the most out of your data.

State-of-the-art algorithms

Apply patient motion correction, calculate contrast uptake curves and pharmacokinetic parameters and visualize this information on top of your images directly in your browser.

Improved workflow

Fetch images from PACS, draw Regions of Interest (ROIs) and have Dynamika automatically calculate statistics for each ROI and export them to PDF, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel format.
