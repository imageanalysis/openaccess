Workflow

Example: How to analyse a dynamic contrast enhanced MR image of breast cancer.

  • Step 1

    Upload data

    Load your images into dynamika from your computer or PACS. Built-in regulatory compliant patient anonymisation ensures you (and your IT department) never have to worry about sensitive patient data leaving your computer.

  • Step 2

    Motion correction

    Dynamika will automatically correct images so that patient motion during image acquisition does not affect subsequent analysis.

  • Step 3

    Draw ROIs

    Dynamika will automatically identify and label contrast uptake patterns. The colour-coded contrast uptake overlays help you to accurately identify pathological tissue which dramatically speeds up drawing 2D and 3D Regions of Interest (ROIs).

  • Step 4

    Download reports

    Dynamika will automatically calculate statistics for all your ROIs and generate reports in PDF, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel format for easy subsequent analysis.
