Workflow

Example: How to analyse a dynamic contrast enhanced MR image of breast cancer.

  • Step 1

    Upload data

    Load you MRI studies into dynamika directly from your browser or PACS. Dynamika comes with built-in regulatory compliant de-identification of DICOM tags so you (or your IT support) never have to worry about sensitive patient data leaving your browser.

  • Step 2

    Motion correction and parametric maps

    Dynamika will automatically correct for patient motion, calculate parametric maps and display the information using image overlays.

  • Step 3

    Draw regions of interest (ROIs)

    Quickly draw 2D and 3D ROIs and have Dynamika automatically calculate statistics for all your ROIs. The parametric map overlays help you to accurately identify pathological tissue.

  • Step 4

    Download reports

    Dynamika will generate reports in PDF, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel format for easy subsequent analysis.
