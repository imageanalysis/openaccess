Workflow

Example: How to analyse a Dynamic Contrast Enhanced MRI (DCE-MRI) of an inflamed joint with Rheumatoid Arthritis.

  • Step 1

    Upload data

    Load your DICOM images into Dynamika from your computer or PACS. Built-in regulatory compliant patient de-identification ensures that you never have to worry about sensitive patient data leaving your hospital.

  • Step 2

    Motion correction

    Dynamika will automatically correct images so that patient motion during image acquisition does not affect subsequent analysis.

  • Step 3

    Draw ROIs

    Dynamika will automatically identify and label contrast uptake patterns. Colour-coded parametric maps help you to accurately detect and differentiate tissue which dramatically speeds up drawing 2D and 3D Regions of Interest (ROIs).

  • Step 4

    Download reports

    Sensitive imaging biomarkers are automatically generated for all ROIs and reported in PDF, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel format for easy subsequent analysis or patient monitoring.
